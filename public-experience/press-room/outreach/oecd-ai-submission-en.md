**Channel:** OECD.AI Catalogue of Tools & Metrics for Trustworthy AI — official submission form at
oecd.ai (the OECD.AI incidents monitor already tracks the Argentine debate this platform addresses).

**Name:** BAFLP — Buenos Aires Framework for Artificial Legal Personhood
**Type:** Open research platform + public-consultation instrument (governance)
**URL:** https://lucianoschadler44-dot.github.io/baflp/ · Repo: github.com/lucianoschadler44-dot/baflp
**Description (short):** Open, versioned, trilingual (EN/ES/PT) research platform on the legal
personhood of AI-operated entities. Six canonical requirements (identity, continuity, registry,
governance, liability, audit) in plain language and academic depth; a jurisdiction observatory
(verified Argentina 2026 case study); a worldwide, fully auditable public consultation (one
account = one vote, comments and reactions merged) compiled daily into a Living Manifesto by
in-repo automation; and a working reference instance — a real AI-operated newsroom with declared
responsibility and public audit. All content CC BY 4.0; machine-readable via /llms.txt.
**Relevance to OECD AI Principles:** accountability, transparency, traceability, human-centred
governance of autonomous economic agents.
