## Summary
Populates the six seed concepts (Artificial Identity, Legal Continuity, Artificial Registry,
Governance, Liability, Audit) with first-draft multi-lens content and declares their relations.

## Motivation
The platform is live; empty concept pages block every downstream surface (glossary, cards,
learning center, simulator). Per the Chief Editor's automation policy (ADR-0001 §13), lenses are
auto-drafted and human-edited afterwards.

## Proposal
Relations: identity depends on registry; continuity depends on identity; liability depends on
registry; governance and audit are mutually reinforcing; audit is what makes liability adjudicable.

Six stubs gain bodies (status: draft, version 0.2.0). No renames, no deletions.
