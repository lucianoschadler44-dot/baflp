# Repository Philosophy

The BAFLP is a permanent research framework, not a publication. We optimise for **ten years**:
maintainability, transparency, reproducibility, auditability and academic credibility.

- **Single source of truth.** The repository is canonical. Outputs (site/PDF/DOCX/EPUB) are generated.
- **Knowledge objects, not chapters.** Each concept exists once and is referenced everywhere.
- **Never overwrite.** Version and archive; history is permanent.
- **Engineering ≠ doctrine.** Engineering builds the rails; the Chief Research Architect lays the track.

Think like the Linux Kernel, ISO, the W3C and an international research institute.
